Ingredients

  • Rice (leftover or boiled the day before)
  • Two Eggs (beaten)
  • Peas
  • Soy Sauce
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat a hot wok on a high heat until smoking, then add the butter
  2. Put the rice and peas into the pan and cook for four minutes
  3. Add the soy sauce
  4. Move the ingredients in the pan to the outer edge revealing the bottom of the pan. Pour the beaten eggs into the dip and allow to cook for one minute
  5. Once the eggs are half set (but still slightly wet) mix the whole pan into one
  6. Serve
