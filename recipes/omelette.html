Ingredients

  • Three Eggs
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • A Splash of Milk
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs in a bowl and add the milk
  2. Heat the pan on a moderate heat and add the butter once warmed
  3. Pour the egg into the pan and allow it to spread evenly, then add the cheese
  4. Once the bottom has mostly set but the centre is still slightly wet use a spatula to fold the omelette in half
  5. Allow to cook for a further few minutes until the egg interior is cooked
  6. Serve
